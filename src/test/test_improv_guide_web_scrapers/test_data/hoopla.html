    improv comedy club

    Hoopla present a selection of the best improv comedy in London.

    Hoopla is the UK and London’s 1st ever improv theatre!

    At least two different London improv groups each night so we can showcase the huge variety of improv styles in London’s flourishing live scene.

    Our London improv comedy club is recommended by Time Out, The Londonist, The Evening Standard and The Daily Telegraph.

    We support all types of improv and have a variety of styles of improv each night including short-form games, scenes, long-form, improvised plays, improvised films and improv musicals.

    We also run improv classes in London for you to try improv yourself, with our beginners improv course as the best starting point, as well as stand up comedy courses, sketch comedy courses and corporate training.
